    • Gaming

    I am Innocent is a mobile app game where you get caught up in a murder mystery and are forced to play detective to figure out an on-going crime. The kidnapper/killer contacts you, and through a mix of texting, real photos, audio (via actual phone calls/recordings), and gaming mechanics (puzzles in particular), you try to solve what's happening while dealing with personal issues, as well. This game is a perfect blend of various media working together to tell one cohesive story.

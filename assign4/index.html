    • Harry Potter

    As a child, I was not an avid reader. It wasn't until I was about 14 that I discovered Harry Potter. The series, which would eventually have seven books, had only released the first four up to that point. I fell in love not only with the series, but also with reading, to the point I became an English teacher and, as I am currently, an elementary school librarian. In short, Harry Potter was and still is an important interest to me--so much so I have introduced it to my 9-year-old, and she has also gained interest. This primary function of this website is to give basic information about each installment of the book series and film counterparts.

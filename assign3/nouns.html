    • Nouns

    Nouns are persons, places, or things. These are almost always things you can see or touch. In a sentence, nouns are the subject and/or object--the things the sentence is about. For example, look at the following sentences:

    See Spot Run.

    The dog is thirsty.

    Where is the bathroom?

    My name is John.

    In these sentences, Spot, dog, bathroom, name, and John are all nouns. Without nouns, we would not know who, what, or where a sentence is about. The following table gives more examples of nouns:

    Person John Sam Carlos Susan Penelope Addison boss manager president boy girl
    Place work park school Texas Mexico Canada kitchen house store bathroom New York City
    Thing car heart lemon pencil chair toilet movie bed cat sign book

    • Music

    Music can be both an integral and often overlooked aspect of transmedia storytelling. It could be something simple like Beyonce's Lemonade, an album that can be viewed in a feature-length video or as an audio album; something far more complex, such as the narrative of modern music, racial history, dance, imagery, and political atmosphere in the United States culminating in Childish Gambino's This is America; or something lighter, like My Chemical Romance's Danger Days album being expanded on in comic book format.

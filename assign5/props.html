    • Props

    Props vary from genre to genre and are usually theme-specific, so it would be too much to get into every single type of prop in escape room scenarios. However, there are common types of locks and other devices used across the board. Types of locks include the following:

    • Combination (both old school rotary and briefcase style)
    • Directional
    • Key/Padlock
    • Lockbox
    • Magnetic
    • Touchpad
    • Word

    There are other commonly used props for puzzles, as well. These include the following:

    • Blacklight flashlight
    • Books/Journals
    • Coats (always check pockets!)
    • Maps
    • Mechanical Devices
    • Photographs
    • Postcards
    • Rope
    • Vials
    • Whiteboards

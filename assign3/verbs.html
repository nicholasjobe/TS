    • Verbs

    Verbs show the action of the sentence. Verbs answer "how?" Without verbs, the nouns have no purpose. For example, look at the following sentences:

    See Spot Run.

    The dog is thirsty.

    Can you show me the bathroom?

    She jumps over the log.

    In these sentences, run, is, show, and jumps are all verbs. To have a complete sentence, there has to be a subject (noun or pronoun) and a verb. The following table gives more examples of verbs:

    Verbs Example Example Example Example Example Example Example Example Example Example Example Example
    Action run jump play work sleep drive cook throw leave go walk slide
    Being is am are was were be being been * * * *
    Linking appear become feel grow look seem remain smell sounds stay taste turn
    *There are only 8 Being verbs and 12 Linking verbs.

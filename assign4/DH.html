    • The Book

    Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows is the seventh and final book in the franchise. It takes place almost entirely outside of Hogwarts, with the exception of the third act and climax.

    Click the button if you don't mind spoilers

    Important Kid Characters Introduced: n/a.

    Important Adult Characters Introduced: Xenophilius Lovegood and Gellert Grindelwald (both mentioned earlier, first time seen).

    Important Non-Human Characters Introduced: Gringotts' dragon (mentioned earlier, first time seen).

    Important Objects Introduced: The Deathly Hallows, though technically all 3 had been introduced already.

    Important Places Introduced: Malfoy Manor, Shell Cottage, and Limbo.

    The Movie

    Deathly Hallows is the first and only book in the franchise to be split into two films (though many concede Goblet of Fire should have been, as well). Both films are highly regarded and include most important details from the book outside of Dumbledore's personal history, which is delved into quite a bit in the book.
